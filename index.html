Money in Roblox and Gift cards

The money that they use in Roblox is called Robux. Some people call it rebex. You can buy it using USD dollars. Gift cards are cards that cost money and when you redeem it with codes, you get robux and items. Ex: $10 equals 800 robux.

Friends in Roblox

You can play Roblox as multiplayer. This means that you can play with online people, friends, or family. The max friends you can have in one account is up to 200 friends. Some people may hack it to have 200+ friends plus.

Hackers in Roblox
